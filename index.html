Feel Healthy

Yoga is an Indian, multi-thousand-year-old philosophy of life and health science that strives for the individual to find peace and inner satisfaction regardless of life's shifts. In the West, we often measure our value in how much we perform. That endeavor may seem good but creates a stress inside that in the long run causes health problems. Stress is not only a mental condition but also greatly affects the physical body with, among other things, tension pain, weakened immune system and poor nutrition.

Good for your heart

Yoga has long been known to lower blood pressure and slow the heart rate. A slower heart rate can benefit people with high blood pressure or heart disease, and people who've had a stroke.

Less stress, more calm

You may feel less stressed and more relaxed after doing some yoga. Some yoga styles use meditation techniques that help calm the mind.

Breathing benefits

Yoga usually involves paying attention to your breath, which can help you relax. It may also call for specific breathing techniques.
