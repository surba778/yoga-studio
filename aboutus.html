About Us

We believe in living a life full of joy, gratitude, and free of judgement. These feelings inform and guide our core teachings and practices at Chennai Yoga Studio.

We focus on helping clients find a deeper connection with themselves and their surroundings.

We do this by offering unique Vinyasa style Yoga classes, kids yoga, teacher trainings for the serious yogi, and yoga retreats for the fun-loving yogi.

Come join this incredible family and experience the ancient art of yoga in a way that will leave you changed forever!
